Custom Rifle Builds

Purpose-built rifles designed around your intended use case - hunting, competition, or duty applications. We’ll discuss goals, budget, timeline, and component selection before work begins.

  • Component selection: barrel, action, trigger, stock/chassis
  • Build planning and clear communication throughout the entire process
  • Final function check with proof of accuracy target
  • Optional accessories including optics, optic mounts/rings, muzzle devices and bipods
