Custom Rifle Builds

Purpose-built rifles designed around your intended use case - hunting, competition, or duty applications. We’ll discuss goals, budget, timeline, and component selection before work begins.

  • Component selection: barrel, action, trigger, stock/chassis
  • Build planning and clear communication throughout the entire process
  • Final function check with proof of accuracy target
  • Optional accessories including optics, optic mounts/rings, muzzle devices and bipods

Firearm Sales and Transfers

Have your firearm purchases delivered to our convenient central Plano location for transfer. Please submit a Firearm Transfer Request prior to placing your order for our shipping address and FFL information. We may also be able to match or beat a competitor’s pricing on customer orders.

  • Open for transfers by appointment only
  • Conveniently available after work hours
  • Ability to custom order firearms by request
  • In your inquiry please include: what firearm is being transferred, shipping FFL’s contact info (Name, Email, Address, Phone number)
  • Please contact us for updated pricing on transfers
  • All sales and transfers subject to a completed 4473 and FBI NICS background check (unless otherwise exempt due to Texas LTC)
